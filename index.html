Rules

The objective of the game is to take the last ball on the board.

You can take any number of balls but you can only take from one line at a time.

To take the balls simply click on any ball from any collumn and the previous balls (highlighted) will be removed

After you click a ball your turn will have ended and the other play's turn will start.
